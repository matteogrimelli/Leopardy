How it works ?

Create a custom game board in Create, save it as a JSON file, and use it in Play to challenge your friends.
Answers are revealed only on request. Keep track of the scoreboard for some extra competition!

Why it works ?

Disability ready

Even your handless friend can play Leopardy, thanks to the insane technology behind this game. Even if you don't have an eye you will still be able to play this game; if you don't have both, damn.

Free to play

Leopardy is completely free to play! Create, customize, and enjoy your game without any costs or subscriptions. Just set up your board and have fun. Or if you want pay me with some V-bucks

Party popper

Leopardy is the perfect party game! Play with friends, share your screen, and enjoy a fun and interactive quiz experience together. Great for game nights and group challenges.
